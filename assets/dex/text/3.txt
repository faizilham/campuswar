<b> El Robo The Robonastran </b><br/>
A Campusian who are interested in the field of electricity, The Robonastran is one unique race of Campusian. Live in both high and watery region, this race has gain the mastery of electrical production and exploitation.The Robonastran was once a peaceful race, long before the war started. When other races came to their region, The Robonastran was one of only a handful races that welcome their visitor warmly. As the visitor then showing their true intentions of occupying their resources, only then the Robonastra stand and show their true power. With their mightiest warrior who are able to bend electricity at his will, El Robo, The Robonastran power in the war is a sight to behold.
